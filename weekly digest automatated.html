    “I can’t build externally what I haven’t built internally.” Dr. Cornel West This is an invitation for all folks who identify as white and are striving toward an identity of anti-racist. This is a space to gather and process the complexity of what it means to be white identifying human beings at this time with a focus on building a competent and firm foundation of anti-racism. This is a supportive and safe space. We will make mistakes. We will have all the best intentions and still have a negative impact. This is a space to learn and grow together, that our intentions may align with our actions, that we may shift to being influential as opposed to impactful. We will bring all our humanness to the table and hold space for all of it.

  • HD Day: Radical Rest

    if you are anything like me, you have spent A LOT of time during the pandemic judging yourself for your productivity (or supposed lack thereof). many of us often gauge our worthiness and our "goodness" by how much we accomplish and push ourselves to work more and grind harder. this summer I discovered Tricia Hersey's Nap Ministry an organization that examines the liberating power of naps (please check it out @thenapministry). the ministry encourages us to see naps as a form of resistance and a powerful tool for healing - especially for BIPOC who have been exploited and enslaved for labor and work for generations. during this pandemic it is essential that we all cultivate a deeper relationship with REST, and recognize that caring for ourselves is vital, in order to honor our own needs and also so that we have the strength to advocate and fight for the rights and freedoms of others. please join us for this day of community rest.

    https://sfuhs.instructure.com/courses/1910/pages/hd-days
